Spaghetti

What is it?

Sweet tasty spaghetti for all to enjoy! Italians would be proud.

What you will need

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

These are optional.

  • 1/2 pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricoota cheese
